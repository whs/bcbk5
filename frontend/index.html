Barcamp Bangkhen?

Barcamp Bangkhen is an annual unconference which attracts over 500 participants from various internet communities to contribute over 40 sessions and learn new things together. We provide venue, t-shirts, lunch and party, but can only do so with assistance from Kasetsart University and our sponsors.

→ See how it works

What is Barcamp?

Barcamp is an ad-hoc unconference born from the desire for people to share and learn in an open environment. It is an intense event with discussions, demos and interaction from attendees. Anyone with something to contribute or with the desire to learn is welcome and invited to join.

→ Official Barcamp website

No spectators, only participants

Attendees must give a demo, a session, or help with one, or otherwise volunteer/contribute in some way to support the event. All presentations are scheduled the day they happen. Prepare in advance, but come early to get a slot on the wall. The people present at the event will select the demos or presentations they want to see.
